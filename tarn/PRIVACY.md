# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 2026
**App:** Tarn

## Summary

Tarn collects no data. Zero. Nothing leaves your device.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect:**
- Personal information
- Health data
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Any data whatsoever

## Data Storage

All data you enter is:
- Stored locally on your device only
- Encrypted with AES-256 using a key derived from your PIN
- Never transmitted anywhere
- Never backed up to any cloud service

## Network Activity

Tarn makes **zero network requests**. The app functions entirely offline. There are no servers, no APIs, no analytics, no telemetry.

## Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties because we do not have access to your data. Your data exists only on your device, encrypted with a key only you know.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings. The app also includes automatic data deletion after failed PIN attempts for your protection.

## Children's Privacy

Tarn does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this document. The app itself will always maintain zero data collection.

Questions? Open an issue at: https://github.com/tarn-app/tarn/issues
